Our Gaming Story

Passionate about gaming since 2010

Who We Are

Founded by a gaming enthusiast, McHenry Gaming Store started as a small local shop in Brighton and has grown into one of the UK's premier gaming destinations. What began as a passion project has transformed into a thriving business serving gamers nationwide.

Our story is rooted in community. From day one, we have believed that gaming is not just about consoles, PCS, or collectables—it is about the people who bring them to life. Whether you are a casual player, a competitive esports enthusiast, or a retro collector, you will find a place here. We are more than a store; we are a gathering spot for those who live and breathe gaming.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality and connection. We carefully curate our products, from the latest AAA titles to rare indie gems, ensuring every gamer finds something to love. However, we also go further—hosting tournaments, launch events, and meetups to unite players. For us, it is not just about selling games but celebrating them.

Our team is made up of gamers, for gamers. Every staff member shares your passion, offering expert recommendations, tech support, and a friendly face to discuss the latest releases. We take pride in being a store where customers become regulars, and regulars become friends.

At McHenry Gaming Store, we are here to fuel your gaming journey. Whether you are building your first setup, hunting for a nostalgic classic, or looking for your next online squad, we have your back. This is where gaming culture thrives—and we cannot wait for you to be part of it.
